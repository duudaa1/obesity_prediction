Understanding Obesity

Obesity is a complex condition that goes beyond weight. It involves biological, behavioral, and emotional factors. It is a chronic, progressive, and recurrent disease, which means it has no cure and tends to worsen over time if left untreated.

Take care of your health mindfully: with the right treatment, it is possible to manage obesity, live better, and prevent serious complications. Don’t ignore the signs — your body deserves care!

Our Reeducation Program

We approach obesity in a comprehensive manner, working on three fundamental pillars:

Nutrition

🍎

What leads to obesity:

Excessive consumption of ultra-processed foods, nutrient-poor diets, and hormonal imbalances.

Risks:

  • Diabetes
  • Hypertension
  • Fatty liver disease

Essential care:

  • Prioritize whole foods
  • Practice mindful eating
  • Maintain proper hydration

Physical

🏃

What leads to obesity:

Sedentary lifestyle, reduced muscle mass, and slow metabolism.

Risks:

  • Joint pain
  • Sleep apnea
  • Poor circulation

Essential care:

  • 150 minutes of moderate activity/week
  • Adapted exercises for your condition
  • Prioritize sleep quality

Mental

🧠

What leads to obesity:

Chronic stress, anxiety, binge eating, and low self-esteem.

Risks:

  • Social isolation
  • Depression
  • Eating disorders

Essential care:

  • Practice self-compassion
  • Identify emotional triggers
  • Seek professional support
